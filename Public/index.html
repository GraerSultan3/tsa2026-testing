Habitat For Humanity

Bryan is host to a local chapter of the global non-proft housing organization, Habitat for Humanity. Bryan Habitat for Humanity is dedicated to constructing reliable housing for families and especially those apart of the Bryan Independent School Distircit. The support provided to students beyond the class room was recently exemplified by the building of a home for the Quintero-Mendez family in 2023. WIth 3 generations of Bryan residents, the community came together to provide for their own.

“Most importantly, Habitat for Humanity was founded on the conviction that every man, woman and child should have a simple, durable place to live in dignity and safety, and that decent shelter in decent communities should be a matter of conscience and action for all.”

Habitat For Humanity's Website

Project Unity

With the motto, “Brazos Valley Gives,” Project Unity promises to serve the greater Bryan area through a variety of social services such as Job Training and Employment as well as Family and Youth Success Programs. They are a tax exempt organization that is dedicated to improving the quality of life of the people in domain they serve. The essence of the organszation is served by the individuals and families of Bryan to promote an intimate relationship among neighbors

Project Unity's Website

Byran Adult Learning Centers

Located at our local community college, Blinn, just 5 minutes from Hstoric Downtown, an Adult Learning Center is hosted to provide tutoring as well as high school GED testing. In an effort to provide uneducated adults wth oppurtunties for self advancement, the ALC is an investment back into the citizens of Bryan rather than outside of it. The GEDs obtained at Blinn open a host opportunities into the careers that will ensure the growth of Bryan.
